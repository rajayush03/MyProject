Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our video consultation service

What equipment do I need for a video consultation?

You'll need a device with a camera and microphone (smartphone, tablet, or computer), a stable internet connection, and a quiet, well-lit place for your consultation.

How long does a typical video consultation last?

Most consultations last 15-30 minutes, depending on the complexity of your health concerns.

Can doctors prescribe medication during a video consultation?

Yes, doctors can prescribe medications during a video consultation. The prescription will be sent electronically to your preferred pharmacy.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule my consultation?

You can cancel or reschedule your consultation up to 2 hours before the appointment without any fees. Please contact our customer service or use the links in your confirmation email.
